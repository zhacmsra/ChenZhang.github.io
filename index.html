About Me

I am a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research Asia. I received my Ph.D. from Center for Energy-efficient Compiting and Applications, Peking University in 2017, advised by Prof. Jason Cong and Prof. Guangyu Sun. I was also a recipient of Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship (2015). Before that, I received my B.E. in Micro-electronics from University of Electronic and Science Technology of China in 2012.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computer architecture, with special focuses on deep learning acceleration, energy-efficient and high performance computing systems.
